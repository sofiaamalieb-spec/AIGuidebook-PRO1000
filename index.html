Quick Tips for Responsible AI Use

  • ✓ Always cite when you use AI tools in your work
  • ✓ Verify facts - AI can make mistakes or "hallucinate" information
  • ✓ Protect privacy - Never share sensitive personal information with AI
  • ✓ Use AI as a tool - Not a replacement for your own learning and thinking
